Kommanda – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Kommanda is a dual-pane file manager designed for macOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data responsibly. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Kommanda does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All operations happen locally on your device.

2. Local File Access

The app accesses files and folders only when you select them or grant permission. File data is used solely to display directory contents and perform your actions (copy, move, delete, rename, preview).

No file contents or metadata are sent anywhere.

3. Internet Usage

Kommanda does not require an internet connection and does not send data over the internet.

4. Third-Party Services

Kommanda does not use analytics services, tracking SDKs, advertising networks, or cloud storage.

5. Security

Since all data stays on your device, your information remains entirely under your control. Kommanda does not receive or store any files or usage logs.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if required by Apple or for transparency improvements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
